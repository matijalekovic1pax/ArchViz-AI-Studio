Quick start
Your first render in 60 seconds
From sign-up to rendered image in four steps.
1
Sign in
Log in with your Google account. No installation needed — everything runs in the browser.
2
Choose a mode
Select a generation mode from the top bar. Start with 3D Rendering or Generate from Text.
3
Upload & configure
Drag your image onto the canvas. Set style, lighting, and camera angle in the left panel. Click Generate.
4
Download or iterate
Save as PNG or JPG from the top bar, or pass the result into another mode to keep refining.
💡New accounts receive a free credit allowance. No credit card required to try the platform.

Generation modes
All 17 modes
Each mode is a specialized pipeline with its own inputs, controls, and output type.

3D Rendering
Transforms a raw 3D model screenshot into a photorealistic architectural render.
Free

Input

A screenshot from Blender, SketchUp, Rhino, Revit, or any 3D tool. Wireframe, clay, or textured views all work. White-background clay renders give the best geometry extraction.

CAD to Render
Turns flat CAD drawings, floor plans, and section cuts into photorealistic renders.
Free

Upload a CAD export (PNG or JPG). The AI reads the line-work and renders it in full 3D. Works with floor plans, elevations, and sections.

SettingOptions
Drawing typeFloor plan · Elevation · Section · Axonometric
Output viewExterior · Interior · Aerial
StyleAll 30+ architectural styles
ℹ️Clean, high-contrast line-work on white produces significantly better results than scanned sketches with noise.
✏️
Sketch to Render
Takes a hand-drawn or digital concept sketch and generates a photorealistic render.
Free

For early design-phase ideation. Even rough pencil sketches produce useful results. The less detail, the more creative freedom the AI takes.

💡Enable Structure adherence: High in the right panel for fidelity to your sketch. Set to Low for creative interpretation.
💬
Generate from Text
No image needed — describe a building or space and get a render.
Free

Type a description into the prompt box. The more context you provide (materials, lighting, surroundings, era), the more specific the result.

StyleExample prompt
Briefa brutalist library in a city square
Detaileda Japanese minimalist wooden pavilion, exposed cedar beams, courtyard with bamboo, late afternoon light
Concepta biophilic office tower where every floor has hanging gardens, tropical canopy through floor-to-ceiling glass
🗺️
Masterplan
Generates a photorealistic aerial masterplan render from a satellite image or site diagram.
Free

Upload a base satellite map or hand-drawn site plan. Draw a polygon boundary on the canvas to define your development zone. The AI renders the area with the specified program type and density.

→
Polygon tool. Click to place vertices. Double-click to close. Ctrl+Z undoes the last point.
→
Full-image mode. Skip the boundary tool to render the entire image as a masterplan.
🖌️
Visual Edit
Paint a selection mask, describe the change, and the AI re-renders only that region.
Free
ToolBest for
BrushIrregular regions: foliage, curved walls
RectangleWindows, panels, rectangular facades
PolygonComplex but crisp shapes
  • Swap cladding: "Replace with dark brushed steel panels"
  • Change landscape: "Replace lawn with gravel courtyard and low hedges"
  • Update glazing: "Floor-to-ceiling glass with thin aluminium frames"
  • Add greenery: "Hanging planters and climbing plants"
💡Keep selections tight to the target area. Overly large masks can alter regions you didn't intend to change.
💥
Exploded View
Generates a technical exploded axonometric diagram revealing structural layers.
Free

Separates the building into labelled layers (foundation, frame, envelope, interior, roof) with connecting guide lines. Best from isometric or axonometric model views.

✂️
Section Render
Takes a flat section drawing and renders it as a dimensional architectural section perspective.
Free

Upload a 2D section cut. The AI adds depth, materials, interior details, lighting, and human scale figures. Best with clearly drawn cut lines and poché on cut elements.

🔄
Multi-Angle
Generates several views of the same building simultaneously from a single input image.
Free

Select angles (front, side, rear, aerial, interior) using checkboxes in the left panel. All views share the same style settings for a consistent presentation package.

Image to CAD
Reverse-engineers a photograph into a structured architectural line drawing (SVG / DXF).
Professional+

Traces walls, openings, columns, and major structural elements into clean vector geometry, organized by layer. Importable directly into AutoCAD, Rhino, or Illustrator.

⚠️Output is an AI interpretation, not a precise measurement. Always verify dimensions against original drawings before use in construction documentation.
🧊
Image to 3D
Generates orthographic reference views from a single architectural photograph for 3D reconstruction.
Professional+

Produces front, side, and top reference views for use when rebuilding a model from a photograph. Useful for massing studies and site modelling.

Video Studio
Creates cinematic video walkthroughs from a single still render using Veo 2 or Kling AI.
Pay-per-clip

Video is charged per clip via Stripe, not from your credit balance. Generation runs asynchronously (2–4 min) and delivers a notification when ready.

Motion presetDescription
Fly-throughCamera pushes forward into the scene
OrbitCamera rotates around the central subject
Pull-backCamera retreats to reveal surrounding context
Crane upVertical rise to aerial perspective
CustomDescribe the camera motion in natural language
💡Upscale your still before generating video — clip quality is directly proportional to input render quality.
🔬
Material Validation
Upload a materials schedule and get an AI compliance check with flagged issues.
Professional+

No image required. Paste or upload a materials schedule (PDF, DOCX, or plain text) and describe the design spec. The AI flags non-compliant, mismatched, or problematic materials.

  • Compliant items — confirmed with no issues
  • Flagged items — requiring verification or substitution
  • Suggested alternatives — compliant replacements for flagged items
  • Summary — overall assessment and priority actions
🌍
Document Translate
Translates DOCX and Excel files preserving all formatting, tables, styles, and layout.
Professional+

Designed for architectural specifications, tender documents, and technical reports. Preserves exact document structure — paragraph styles, tables, headers, footers — in the output.

  • DOCX — full formatting preservation
  • XLSX — cell content translated, formulas preserved
  • PDF — converted to DOCX first, then translated (minor formatting loss)
🗜️
PDF Compression
Compresses large architectural PDF sets for email submission or portal upload.
Free

Upload any PDF and select a compression level: Low (max quality), Medium (balanced), or High (minimum file size). Typical architectural drawing sets see 40–70% reduction at Medium.

Best practices
Tips & tricks
💡
Chain modes together. 3D Rendering → Visual Edit → Upscale. Results compound significantly at each step.
💡
Draft at 1K, deliver at 4K. Iterate cheaply at 1K until you're happy with composition and style, then re-run at 4K for the final output.
💡
Describe the photo, not just the building. "Late afternoon golden light, street-level 35mm shot, slight lens flare" produces more atmosphere than a pure architectural description.
💡
White clay renders = best 3D results. Export with no textures on a white background. The AI reads geometry most accurately from clean clay models.
💡
Tight masks for Visual Edit. Constrain selections to exactly the area you want changed — loose masks give the AI too much latitude.
💡
16:9 for boards, 3:4 for social. Match your aspect ratio to the destination — widescreen for presentations, portrait for Instagram and LinkedIn.
💡
Upscale before video. The richer the input still, the more convincing the camera motion in Video Studio.
💡
Use History to A/B test styles. Run the same prompt with two different styles back-to-back, then compare thumbnails in the History panel.
